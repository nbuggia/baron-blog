title: Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

These are likely going to be the best oatmeal raisin cookies you’ve ever had. The secret is toasting the nuts before mixing them into the cookie batter. This gives them a unique and delicious flavor. Other than that, these are you typical moist and delicious oatmeal raisin cookie.

##Ingredients

* 1 cup raw cashews
* 1 cup raw walnuts
* 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
* 1 cup light brown sugar
* 1/2 cup sugar
* 2 eggs
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
* 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1 teaspoon cinnamon
* 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
* 1 teaspoons salt
* 1.5 cups raisins
* 3 cups Quaker Oats

Optional

* 1/3 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
* 1/4 cup dried cranberries
* 1/4 cup of shredded coconuts

##Instructions

* Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
* Toast the nuts – heat these in a sauce pan until lightly browned, then remove and lightly chop
* 1 cup of cashews
* 1 cup of walnuts
* Wet ingredients – in a medium bowl:
* Cream the 1/2 cup unsalted butter
* Add 1 cup brown sugar
* Add 1/2 cup sugar
* Add 2 eggs, one at a time
* Add 1 teaspoon vanilla
* Dry ingredients – in another bowl sift together:
* Add 1 1/2 cups flour
* Add 1 teaspoon baking soda
